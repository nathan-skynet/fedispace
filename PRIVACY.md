## Overview

FediSpace is an open-source Pixelfed client. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data FediSpace collects, how it is used, and your rights.

## Data Collection

### Data We Collect
- **Account credentials**: Your Pixelfed/Mastodon instance URL, username, and OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device to authenticate with your server.
- **App preferences**: Your settings (theme, language, notification preferences) are stored locally on your device.

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal information on any external server.
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking services.
- We do **not** share any data with third parties.
- We do **not** display advertisements.

## AI Features

FediSpace includes optional AI-powered features (translation, image editing). These features:
- Connect to **your own** AI server (Ollama, LM Studio, OpenAI-compatible API) that **you** configure.
- We do **not** provide or operate any AI server.
- All AI processing happens between your device and **your own** configured server.
- No data is sent to our servers.

## Push Notifications

FediSpace supports push notifications via **UnifiedPush**, a decentralized notification protocol.
- Notifications are delivered through a distributor app of **your** choice.
- We do **not** operate any push notification server.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- OAuth tokens
- Cached timeline content
- User preferences and settings

No data is transmitted to or stored on servers operated by FediSpace developers.

## Third-Party Services

FediSpace connects only to:
- The Pixelfed/Mastodon instance **you** choose to log into.
- The AI server **you** optionally configure.
- The UnifiedPush distributor **you** optionally configure.

We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the privacy practices of these third-party services.
